# 🛒 Supermarket Sales Analysis

A data analysis project focused on understanding supermarket sales performance, customer behavior, payment preferences, and branch-level performance using **Excel, SQL, and Python**.


## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes supermarket sales data to identify patterns in product performance, customer activity, branch performance, and purchasing behavior. The objective is to transform raw business data into actionable insights that can support decision-making and improve overall business performance.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze sales performance across product categories.
* Compare branch performance.
* Identify customer activity by city.
* Evaluate payment method preferences.
* Examine spending patterns by gender.
* Generate business insights and recommendations.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

* Microsoft Excel
* SQL (MySQL)
* Python
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Seaborn
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📊 Key Business Questions

1. Which product line performs best?
2. Which branch has the highest sales performance?
3. Which city has the highest customer activity?
4. Which payment method is most preferred?
5. Do male and female customers spend differently?
6. Which product category generates the highest average sales?
7. What business improvements can be recommended?

---

## 📈 Key Findings

### Product Analysis

* Home and Lifestyle recorded the highest average sales.
* Fashion Accessories showed comparatively lower performance.

### Branch Analysis

* Giza achieved the highest average sales among branches.

### Payment Analysis

* Ewallet was the most frequently used payment method.

### City Analysis

* Yangon recorded the highest customer activity.

### Gender Analysis

* Female customers demonstrated higher average spending than male customers.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase inventory allocation for high-performing product categories.
* Introduce promotional campaigns for weaker categories.
* Use top-performing branches as operational benchmarks.
* Encourage digital payments through offers and incentives.
* Develop targeted marketing campaigns based on customer spending behavior.
